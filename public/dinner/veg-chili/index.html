    Vegetable Chili

    No tricky parts to this at all.

    Preparation time
    about 50 mins
    Cooking time
    40 minutes on Stove
    makes 8 chili
    freeze after cooking

    Ingredients

    • Chili base
      • 400g green lentils
      • 3 ribs celery, chopped
      • 4 large carrots, chopped
      • 4 cans chopped tomatoes , liquidized if required
      • 3 cans of your favorite beans (one should really be Kidney beans )
    • Seasoning ( to taste)
      • 2 teaspoons turmeric
      • 1 teaspoons of cumin
      • 2 oxo cubes
      • 2 teaspoons mixed spice
      • 1/2 teaspoons black pepper
      • 1/2 teaspoons cayenne pepper

    Method

    Base Mix

    Step 1) Add the lentils to a pan once rinsed and then bring to the boil with 500 ml water and boil for 10 minutes.

    Once boiled then leave to simmer for 30 minutes until soft. Best top use Green Lentils as they work really well with Chili as they hold together well.

    Step 2) add the chopped the tomatoes to a large pan and liquidize as required.

    Step 3) add the chopped carrots and celery

    Step 4) add the chopped Onion, I chop mine up in a food processor as I dont like large bits on Onion

    Step 5) add all the chopped ingredients along with the cooked Lentils to the large pan

    Step 6) add all the Seasoning to the large pan

    Step 7) Stir well and cook on a low heat for 10 minutes or so

    Step 4) serve with rice or CHIPS!

    Item Value per Chili (no Rice)
    Calories 317
    Protein 20g
    Carbohydrates 45g
    Fiber 18g
    Fat 2g of which 1g is saturated


    Clearly you can just increase the proportions to make an even larger batch !
